About Me

I was born in Grand Rapids, Michigan. When I was five years old my dad took a job in Bettendorf, Iowa and I've been stuck in Iowa ever since. I have one younger sister named Chelsea. We both graduated high school from Pleasant Valley High School. I played tennis for the high school team and played basketball everyday. I started my working career with a lawn mowing business with a friend when we were twelve years old. I then became a baseball umpire when I was fourteen years old. When I turned sixteen I started working at a Suzuki car dealership as a detailer until I graduated high school and moved away for college. After six months in college I took a position with Northwest Airlines who eventually merged with Delta Airlines for which I currently work. I enjoy traveling and have been very fortunate with the opportunites provided by my employer.

I am currently a senior MIS major. I have become very accustomed to my flight benefits working for the airlines. I am hoping to move to a position in either Delta or another major airlines IT department. One of my favorite hobbies is snowboarding and I would like to be able to travel to as many different winter destinations around the world as possible.
